📢 *Gate Access App – Lakeridge Community*

🔗 Open this link:
https://lakeridge.qrgate.in

📲 *Step 1: Install App*
• Android: 3 dots (⋮) → Add to Home Screen
• iPhone: Open in Safari → Share (⬆️) → Add to Home Screen

📱 *Step 2: Open Gate App*
Tap the app icon from your mobile

🧾 *Step 3: One-Time Setup*
Enter your *Mobile Number* and *Secret Key* → Tap *Save*

🎫 *Step 4: Generate QR*
Tap *Generate QR* and show/share with visitor

⏳ *Important*
• QR valid for *30 minutes*
• QR works *only once*
• Device will be locked after first use

📤 *Sharing*
Tap Share → WhatsApp
If QR not attached, take screenshot and share

🚗 At gate: Show QR → scan → gate opens

🙏 For help, contact Association
